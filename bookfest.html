Join us for the BOOKBOUND FESTIVAL 2024 on 15-10-2024 at KAMARAJ ARANGAM ! Celebrate literature with bestselling authors, exciting panels, and book signings.

On October 15, 2024, Chennai will host its annual book festival, a vibrant celebration of literature and creativity that promises to captivate book lovers of all ages. The festival will feature renowned authors, engaging panel discussions, and book signings, alongside workshops that encourage aspiring writers to hone their craft. With stalls showcasing a diverse range of genres from fiction to non-fiction, attendees will have the opportunity to explore new titles and meet fellow literature enthusiasts. Local publishers and independent bookstores will also play a key role, promoting regional voices and fostering a love for reading in the community. This festival is set to be a delightful gathering that highlights the rich literary culture of Chennai

Keynote Speakers: Hear from Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie, Neil Gaiman, Elizabeth Acevedo, Colson Whitehead, N.K. Jemisin
Workshops: Join interactive sessions on writing and publishing
Networking Opportunities: Connect with fellow book lovers
